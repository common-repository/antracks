== Description ==
This is a youtube playlist plugin.

== Installation ==
1.) Download the zip file.<br>
2.) On your dashboard, click on plugins and click add new. Then, click on Upload then choose the zip file downloaded from http://wordpress.org/plugins/antracks/. Then click "install now".<br>
3.) Click on Plugins from your dashboard and activate Antracks.<br>
4.) Go to your wordpress folder->wp-content->plugins->newantracks.<br>
5.) Open newantracks.php<br>
6.) On line 26 of newantracks.php, edit the localhost, username, and password to your corresponding database localhost name, username, and password<br>
7.) To add playlists on your database, go to your wordpress dashboard and click on Settings.<br>
8.) Under the Settings, click on antracks. There you can record playlist on your database.<br>
9.) On the antracks admin page, fill in your desired playlist name and copy paste your chosen playlist link. Then click submit.<br>
10.) After submitting your playlist entry, choose the playlist on the dropdown menu and choose the autoplay option. After that, click submit and a text area will appear showing the shortcode.<br>
11.) The shortcode looks like this: [antracks id='playlist name']. Copy and paste this shortcode to your page/post and save.<br>
12.) View the page to which your playlist was embedded.<br>
